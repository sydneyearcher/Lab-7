Here are some interesting facts about Nebraska!

1.In 1927, Edwin E. Perkins of Hastings invented the kid-favorite powdered drink, Kool-Aid.

2.Nebraska has more miles of rivers than any other state.

3.Nebraska has the worlds largest indoor rain forest: The Lied Jungle at Henry Doorly Zoo in Omaha

4.Nebraska's Chimney Rock was the landmark most often mentioned in journal entries by travelers on the Oregon Trail.

5.The Naval Ammunition Depot in Hastings was the largest U.S. ammunition plant, providing 40% of WWII's ammunition.
